Heading: 5 different kinds of identifier

Here they are:

  1. PIN
    Personal Identification number: a number allocated to an individual and used to validate electronic transactions.
    E.g: 001099003663
  2. Email address
    An email address is a unique identifier for an email account. It is used to both send and receive email messages over the Internet.
    E.g: hung.nt176773@sis.hust.edu.vn
  3. Phone number
    A number assigned to a telephone line for a specific phone or set of phones (as for a residence) that is used to call that phone.
  4. Signature
    A person's name written in a distinctive way as a form of identification in authorizing a check or document or concluding a letter.
  5. License plate number
    A sign affixed to a vehicle displaying a series of letters or numbers indicating that the vehicle has been registered with the state.
